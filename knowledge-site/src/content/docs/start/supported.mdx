## Regions
The only region currently supported is __Malaysia__. 

:::note
Companies based outside of Malaysia are still able to register for Leanx, with the condition of being legally registered in Malaysia via Companies Commission of Malaysia / Suruhanjaya Syarikat Malaysia (SSM). Click <a href="https://www.ssm.com.my/Pages/Home.aspx" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a> to register your business!
:::
