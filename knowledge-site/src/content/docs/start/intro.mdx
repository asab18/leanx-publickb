## What is LeanX?
We bring together everything that's necessary to build a good online business, from accepting payment, sending money nationwide or selling products in the internet. Lean.x's product suite empower all type of businesses, whether it is an online e-commerce business or a giant internet corporation, long established businesses or newly registered businesses.

Our mission is simple - to make all your business payment processes SMARTER and SIMPLER.

## LeanX Products
LeanX is a next-generation unified payment platform that provides comprehensive financial technology solutions for businesses of all sizes. Built with a technology-focused approach, LeanX offers three core products that work together to streamline payment collection and disbursement processes. Their platform combines robust API infrastructure, user-friendly interfaces, and advanced automation tools to help businesses efficiently manage their entire payment ecosystem from a single, secure platform.

### Cloud Payment
Cloud Payment is a comprehensive payment processing platform that serves as the foundation for online and in-person retailers, subscription businesses, software platforms, and marketplaces.
